The Challenge

Beyond the Organic Funnel:
How Noblegreen Turf Scaled Past a Single Source of Growth

Noblegreen Turf was generating leads, but everything ran through a single organic funnel. One algorithm update, one seasonal dip, and the pipeline slowed. There was no paid layer to catch the slack, no multi-location presence to capture broader demand, and no way to scale without waiting for SEO to compound further.

Growth was happening, but it was fragile. The business needed a system that didn't depend on any single asset, one that could produce consistent, qualified leads regardless of what Google did next.

What We Did

Two Channels.
One Engine.

We didn't just run ads or tweak a website. As a Toronto, Canada marketing agency, Net Leads built a multi-asset lead system from the ground up.

SEO

15-Site Organic Portfolio

We built a network of 15 geo-targeted website assets, each capturing high-intent local search traffic in its own market. Each site ranks independently, so no single algorithm update can wipe out the lead flow. The portfolio creates a diversified organic presence across multiple cities and regions under the Noblegreen umbrella.

Google Ads

Paid Layer on Top

With organic traffic established as the base, we layered Google Ads campaigns to capture demand the moment someone searched. Paid and organic working in the same direction, amplifying each other instead of competing. The result is a consistent lead flow that doesn't wait for rankings and doesn't stop when they shift.

The Results

A Lead Engine That
Never Stops!

The two-channel system now generates 50+ qualified leads every month across the portfolio, consistent, scalable, and not dependent on any single site or ranking.
